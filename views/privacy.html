Transparency and Open Source Commitment

1. Open Source AI Models

LibreAIChat uses open-source AI models from various providers. These models are community-developed, transparent, and not controlled by a single corporate entity.

2. User Discretion

By using LibreAIChat, you acknowledge that:

  • Open-source models may have inherent biases or limitations
  • Responses are generated based on model training and may not always be perfect
  • You are responsible for critically evaluating and using AI-generated content
  • We do not guarantee the absolute accuracy or appropriateness of model outputs

3. Data Privacy

We are committed to:

  • Never storing your conversations
  • Not using your queries to train models
  • Minimal tracking for essential service functionality
  • Complete transparency about our data practices

4. Model Diversity

We provide multiple open-source models to give you choice and flexibility. Each model has its own strengths, and we encourage users to explore and find the most suitable model for their specific needs.

5. Account Data & Deletion

You can permanently delete your LibreAIChat account at any time from your account settings. All associated data, including subscription details and usage stats, will be completely erased from our systems.

6. Payment & Subscription Data

We use Stripe for secure payment processing. We never store your credit card details directly. Subscription-related metadata (like plan, billing date, and status) is stored securely to manage your account.
