About LibreAIChat

AI Conversations Made Simple

Our Vision

LibreAIChat was built to make AI accessible to everyone. We created this platform so that anyone — regardless of technical skills or hardware capabilities — can access powerful AI tools with minimal hassle.

Simplicity by Design

We believe that using AI shouldn't require complex setups or powerful hardware. With LibreAIChat, you can start chatting immediately from any device, anywhere.

No downloads, no installations, and no configuration needed — just straightforward access to AI assistance.

Advanced AI Made Accessible

LibreAIChat gives you access to modern AI models without requiring you to have specialized knowledge or expensive hardware. We handle the technical aspects so you can focus on getting the answers you need.

Affordable by Design

AI shouldn't cost a fortune. Our pricing starts at $2.99/month — about the price of a cup of coffee — with no upsells, no hidden charges, and full access across tiers.

The free tier lets anyone try the service, and paid plans simply unlock higher usage limits.

Why Libre?

"Libre" reflects our commitment to making AI access straightforward and available to everyone. LibreAIChat is our way of ensuring that advanced AI capabilities remain accessible and easy to use.

Respectful Analytics

To better understand how people use LibreAIChat, we use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first, open-source alternativ e to Google Analytics. It doesn’t use cookies, doesn’t collect personal data, and doesn’t track you across the web.

We believe in transparency, and Plausible helps us measure usage without compromising your privacy.
